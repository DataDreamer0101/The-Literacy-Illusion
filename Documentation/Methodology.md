# Methodology

## Data Cleaning
- Standardized column names across PES and Census datasets
- Handled missing values (marked "-" as 0)
- Calculated transition rates between education levels
- Computed Gender Parity Index (GPI) for each province
- Normalized facility scores (electricity, water, toilets, boundary walls)

## Analytical Steps
1. **Input-Outcome Comparison** – Mapped PES institutional data against Census literacy/attainment
2. **Pipeline Analysis** – Calculated progression from primary → middle → matric → intermediate → graduation
3. **Disparity Mapping** – Provincial, rural/urban, gender, and age-group breakdowns
4. **Correlation Analysis** – Facility quality vs. retention; ICT access vs. literacy
5. **Budget Trend Analysis** – Education expenditure vs. GDP share, development vs. current split
