# The Literacy Illusion: What Pakistan's Education Data Hides

## 📌 Project Overview
Pakistan's official education statistics celebrate rising school counts, enrollment, and teacher numbers. But beneath this progress lies a hidden crisis—millions of students disappear before completing their education. This project uncovers the **"Literacy Illusion"** by cross-referencing Pakistan Education Statistics (PES) with Census 2023 data, revealing a leaking pipeline, deep geographic and gender disparities, and a critical "Matric Cliff" where 60% of students drop out.

## 🎯 Project Goal
To move beyond input-based metrics and provide a **diagnostic analysis** of Pakistan's education system—identifying exactly where, why, and how students are being left behind, and equipping policymakers with an interactive dashboard for targeted intervention.

## 📊 Data Sources
| Source | Years | Key Indicators |
|--------|-------|----------------|
| Pakistan Education Statistics (PES) | 2019-2024 | Institutions, enrollment, teachers, facilities (electricity, water, toilets) |
| Pakistan Census | 2017 & 2023 | Literacy rates, educational attainment, out-of-school children, gender parity, rural/urban breakdown |
| Federal & Provincial Budget Documents | 2018-2023 | Education expenditure, development vs. current budget, GDP share |

## 🔍 Key Findings
- **22.8 million children** out of school – largest in South Asia
- **62% national literacy** hides 48% rural vs 81% urban
- **60% dropout** at the "Matric Cliff" (middle to high school transition)
- **Only 5.28%** of education budget goes to development
- **ICT access** improves outcomes by 81%
- **Urban females** now outperform males in higher education (quiet revolution)
- **Migration patterns** mirror low-literacy districts

## 🚀 How to Use This Repository
1. **Download the Power BI file** from the `PowerBI/` folder.
2. Open with **Power BI Desktop** (free version).
3. Use slicers to filter by **province, district, gender, locality (rural/urban)**.
4. Explore the three dashboards:
   - **Dashboard 1:** Input growth (schools, enrollment, teachers)
   - **Dashboard 2:** Outcome realities (literacy, dropouts, disparities)
   - **Dashboard 3:** Root causes (budget, ICT, migration)

## 📈 Key Visualizations
- **KPI Cards** – Total institutions, enrollment, out-of-school children
- **Choropleth Maps** – Literacy rates by province and district
- **Sankey Diagram** – Educational pipeline (primary to graduation)
- **Clustered Bar Charts** – Provincial comparisons
- **Scatter Plots** – Facilities vs. retention rates
- **Migration Heatmaps** – Out-migration districts vs. low-literacy zones

## 📝 Policy Implications
While our project is **analytical** (not predictive), the data points to three evidence-based directions:
1. **Shift from new construction to facility upgrades** (electricity, water, toilets)
2. **Target the "Matric Cliff"** with conditional scholarships in high-dropout districts
3. **Invest in ICT infrastructure** – schools with digital access show 81% better outcomes
